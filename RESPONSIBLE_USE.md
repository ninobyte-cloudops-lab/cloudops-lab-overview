# Responsible Use

The AI-Native CloudOps Lab is built on a governed, sandbox-first approach. These principles apply to everyone who participates.

## Principles

- **No uncontrolled AWS access.** All practice happens inside a governed sandbox with defined boundaries.
- **No credential sharing.** Access is individual and is never shared, posted, or reused.
- **No public leakage.** Internal materials, lab contents, and any evidence stay private and sanitized.
- **Governed sandbox first.** Guardrails and safety gates precede any live execution.

## What this work does not promise

- No guarantee of certification.
- No guarantee of employment or job placement.
- No claim of AWS partnership or official status.

Practice is designed to build real, demonstrable skill — shown through evidence and proof of work, not through unverifiable claims.
